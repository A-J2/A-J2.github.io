Step 1: Getting Your Car Ready for Export

At AutoTour Afrique, we make the car export process simple, starting with helping you get your vehicle ready to go. We’ll walk you through gathering the important paperwork like the car’s title, bill of sale, and any necessary export permits. Don’t worry, we’ll also make sure your vehicle meets the import regulations of the destination country, from emissions to safety standards. We even advise you on cleaning the car (yep, it’s a thing!) to pass inspections. Plus, we’ll help set up insurance to protect your car while it’s on its way.

Step 2: Shipping Your Car and Handling the Paperwork

Once your car’s good to go, we’ll take care of the shipping details. Whether you choose roll-on/roll-off (RoRo) or container shipping, AutoTour Afrique has trusted partners to ensure your vehicle gets there safely. The paperwork? Leave that to us! We’ll handle everything from export declarations to customs forms, and we’ll make sure your car meets all the import requirements for its new country. We’ll also take care of the taxes and fees, so you don’t have to stress about any customs delays. Your car is in good hands with us!

Step 3: Getting Your Car Ready to Hit the Road

When your car arrives at its destination, AutoTour Afrique is still with you! We’ll help get it through customs quickly and make sure all the duties and taxes are squared away. If the country requires inspections, no problem — we’ll guide you through it. Finally, we’ll assist with registering your vehicle so it’s legally ready to hit the road. From start to finish, we’ve got your back, making sure your car export experience is smooth, stress-free, and, best of all, easy!
